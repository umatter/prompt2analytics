## QUICK WINS (High priority + Simple complexity)

These can be implemented quickly with high impact:

### 1. `var.test` - F Test to Compare Two Variances
**Score:** 95 | **Complexity:** Simple
**Doc:** https://stat.ethz.ch/R-manual/R-devel/library/stats/html/var.test.html
**Description:** Performs an F test to compare the variances of two samples from normal populations.
**Implementation Notes:**
- Requires F distribution (available via `statrs`)
- Simple formula: F = s1²/s2², with df = (n1-1, n2-1)
- Already have `f_test_p_value` helper

### 2. `prop.test` - Test of Equal or Given Proportions
**Score:** 92 | **Complexity:** Simple
**Doc:** https://stat.ethz.ch/R-manual/R-devel/library/stats/html/prop.test.html
**Description:** Tests the null that the proportions in several groups are the same, or that they equal certain given values.
**Implementation Notes:**
- Chi-squared test variant for proportions
- Already have chi-squared infrastructure

### 3. `binom.test` - Exact Binomial Test
**Score:** 90 | **Complexity:** Simple
**Doc:** https://stat.ethz.ch/R-manual/R-devel/library/stats/html/binom.test.html
**Description:** Performs an exact test of a simple null hypothesis about the probability of success in a Bernoulli experiment.
**Implementation Notes:**
- Uses binomial distribution (via `statrs`)
- Exact two-sided, less, or greater alternatives

### 4. `fligner.test` - Fligner-Killeen Test of Homogeneity of Variances
**Score:** 88 | **Complexity:** Simple
**Doc:** https://stat.ethz.ch/R-manual/R-devel/library/stats/html/fligner.test.html
**Description:** Non-parametric test for homogeneity of variances, robust to departures from normality.
**Implementation Notes:**
- Based on ranks, uses chi-squared distribution
- Complement to Bartlett's test (already implemented)

### 5. `ansari.test` - Ansari-Bradley Test

### 6. `mood.test` - Mood Two-Sample Test of Scale

**Description:** Tests the null hypothesis that two samples come from distributions that differ only in location.

### 7. `kruskal.test` - Kruskal-Wallis Rank Sum Test

### 8. `oneway.test` - Test for Equal Means in One-Way Layout

### 9. `mcnemar.test` - McNemar's Chi-squared Test

### `var.test` - F Test to Compare Two Variances
**Category:** Hypothesis Testing
**Priority Score:** 95/100
**Complexity:** Simple
**Documentation:** https://stat.ethz.ch/R-manual/R-devel/library/stats/html/var.test.html
